The CSV time_dynamics_m2.csv file contains the time dynamics of opinions in the simulation.
Columns:
1. Time Step: The time step in the simulation.
2. Opinion Mean: Mean opinion of all agents at that time step.
3. Opinion SD: Standard deviation of opinions of all agents at that time step.
4. Friends Mean: Mean number of friends per agent at that time step.
5. Friends SD: Standard deviation of number of friends per agent at that time step.

Simulation Parameters:
Number of Agents: 10000
Time Steps: 1000
Proportion of Interactions per Time Step: 0.05

Generated by opinion dynamics simulation script.
